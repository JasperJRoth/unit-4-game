Game Rules:
  • To build a building, press the button labelled with the building you want, then click a spot on the board (make sure you have enough money!)
  • Farms create farmers every few turns
  • Farmers move up and down until they reach a market
  • When a farmer reaches a market they generate money for the player that owns them
  • The further the farmer travelled to get to the market, the more money they make
  • it is reccomended to start the game by placing a farm and market on a column
  • Castles create knights every few turns
  • Knights move forward and generate money by pillaging buildings or killing farmers
  • When a knight reaches the end of the board, your opponent loses money
  • When a player has negative money, they lose the game
  • You cannot build on spaces you do not own
  • A player owns all the spaces up to the furthest they own plus two, unless there is a conflict
